05 · Motion

Moves Like Sport

Punchy and physical. Motion is a feature, never decoration — and it fully respects prefers-reduced-motion (the kinetic layer goes static).

01

Springy

Overshoot easing on press & reveal — cubic-bezier(.2,1.3,.3,1). Things land like a footplant.

02

Count-Up

Big numbers race to value on entry. The number IS the moment — let it sprint, then settle.

03

Diagonal Sweep

Gradients and gliders cut across on a slight skew. Energy moves left-to-right, never flat.

04

Restraint

One hero moment per screen. Loud, not messy — kill motion the instant the user opts out.
