Motion & Material Principles

How the glass behaves

01

Depth is real

Layers separate with blur, shadow, and Z-translation. Content lifts toward the light on hover.

02

Floaty & spring

Motion eases in and out, never snaps. Press gives a soft spring; panels gently bob and breathe.

03

Glow signals intent

Cyan glow blooms on focus and primary actions — light, not weight, communicates importance.

04

Legible over glass

Text sits on darker frosted plates where it overlaps imagery, holding AA contrast everywhere.

05

Atmosphere, not flatness

The background is a living dusk mesh of cyan and violet orbs — never a solid fill.

06

Calm by default

Reduced-motion users get a still, legible board: orbs freeze, transitions collapse.
