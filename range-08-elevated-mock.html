3) Tee-off from the very bottom — trajectory origin fixed at (TEE_X, yBottom) = the foreground grass tee, every distance, every variant.

Verification system: window.__rangeCheck() projects every visible grid line and the landing point to screen-Y for each variant at 5 shot distances and asserts each falls inside the photo's measured grass band — see panel below the cards.
