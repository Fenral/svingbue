Growth, Automation & Realistic Revenue

A decision-ready answer to nine owner questions — built on the honest engine, not around it.

2026-07-21 · autonomous strategy pass

The short version

  1. The product is good; the gap is distribution and retention — not the engine. Nothing here changes physics.
  2. Demand is searchable: "why do I slice" is the #1 amateur golf question, and casual/off-course golf is up 40%+ since 2019.
  3. One production → everywhere: make the app produce its own shareable content, caption it with one AI tool, fan out to TikTok + Reels + Shorts from one scheduler.
  4. 1M NOK/yr net is a top-~5% outcome (only 4.6% of new sub-apps reach $10k MRR in 2 yrs) — reachable, not automatic.
  5. Biggest blocker: there is still no native iOS/Android project — nothing to send installs into yet.
  6. Retention low-hanging fruit is cheap because the engine + Academy XP already exist: shot-cards, Diagnose, streaks, seasonal packs.
  7. Big-studio polish is buyable in 2026 — Rive/Lottie, Superwall, Mobbin, and the Figma/Higgsfield tools already wired in here.

The nine questions

Q1

What people search for in golf

Målbilde

A list of high-intent "curiosity" phrases + 5 winning formats, each mapped to an app surface.

The slice is the centre of gravity. "Why does my ball go right / how to stop slicing" is the single most common question in amateur golf — and the answer (face vs. path at impact) is exactly what your engine draws live. Winning formats: hook-first myth-busters, before/after, "so that's why," challenges, relatable.

Q2

How to resonate more, realistically

Målbilde

One sentence a stranger repeats after 5 seconds + the 3 things only you can honestly own.

"Flightglass answers the one question every golfer asks — why did it do that? — with real physics, live." Own: the honest engine, live cause→effect, and "answer your own shot." Resonance comes from legibility of what you have, not new capability.

Not this: external TV shots · measuring the real swing · technique coaching. All break the honesty moat.

Q3

Which social media, and how

Målbilde

One primary engine, one owned funnel, one credibility beachhead — each with a job.

  • Primary: short-form vertical video → TikTok + Reels + Shorts (one post, 3× reach). 3–5×/wk.
  • Owned: email/waitlist you control + link-in-bio.
  • Trust: Reddit / MyGolfSpy / Norwegian PGA — post the explainer, not the app.

The app is the content studio: the tracer bending live, the Diagnose reveal, an Academy diagram.

Q4

Make the marketing near-automatic

Målbilde

One batch day/month + ~20 min/week keeps daily presence, ASO tests & lifecycle all running.

  • Product makes the content (shareable shot-cards) — the supply that feeds everything.
  • Enhance/caption: Submagic (~$20) or OpusClip (~$15); Higgsfield for hero b-roll.
  • Fan out: Metricool (free) or Postiz (free self-host) → all platforms on schedule.
  • ASO auto-tests: Apple Custom Product Pages + Google Listing Experiments (free).
  • Paid, tiny: Apple Search Ads long-tail, $5–10/day. Defer Meta/TikTok to 2027.
  • Lifecycle: OneSignal push + auto review-prompts after the aha.

Stays manual: the hook idea, community replies, reading the data.

Q6

What blocks > a million

Målbilde

The five real constraints, ranked, each with its unlock.

  • 1. No native app yet — no iOS/Android project to acquire installs into. First domino.
  • 2. Cold-start invisibility — seed reviews + content engine.
  • 3. Aha is one-time — needs a recurring reason to return (Diagnose, seasonal).
  • 4. Solo bandwidth — the automation stack exists to solve exactly this.
  • 5. Price left on the table — ship 99/399/999, then A/B forever.

Not the blocker: the engine, the design, the honesty. Those are assets.

Q7

Download-and-test, or real use?

Målbilde

Honest classification + the changes that move it toward habit.

Today it leans "download → get the aha → churn." A revelation is great for acquisition, finite for retention. Design it as a tool you consult (a weather app for your ball flight) — realistic target: consult weekly-to-monthly, driven by Diagnose-after-every-range-session + seasonal content. Truth signal: W4 retention of activated users ≥ 10–15%.
