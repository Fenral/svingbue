Motion Principles

mechanical · fast · honest
P.01

Mechanical Easing

Fast settle, no bounce. cubic-bezier(.2,.9,.2,1). Motion reads as an instrument moving, not a toy.

P.02

Count The Numbers

Numerics count up to their value. The measurement should feel earned, captured, locked.

P.03

Hairline Reveals

Lines sweep and grids draw in. State changes ride the rule, never a fade-and-pop.

P.04

Signal, Not Sparkle

Amber means gain, magenta means fault. Color is data — never used to decorate.

P.05

Calm Under Load

Dense data, quiet surface. Restraint is the premium. ≥44px targets, focus always visible.
