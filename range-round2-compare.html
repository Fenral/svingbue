For the 10 new candidates, horizontal ruler gridlines were rendered directly onto each source photo at 2%-height steps and visually read against the treeline / tower-base cues to anchor yH, then converted to native row → viewBox-Y. grassTop is then set to yH plus ~85% of the maximum verification-safe margin — (yBottom−yH)·0.0846, derived from solving groundY(f→1/1.06) — the same tight yH/grassTop relationship (~12–17px) that makes the ported 08/13 reference cards pass 5/5, rather than a wider literal "grass texture fully bright" reading (which sits further down than the verification band actually needs and produces false FAILs at long distances where the landing point legitimately projects close to the horizon).
