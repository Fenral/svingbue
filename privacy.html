1.An anonymous app

StrikeArc has no account, no login, and no sign-up. We do not collect your name, email address, or any profile information through the app itself. Swing settings, shot history, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to us.

The only data that leaves your device is described in the sections below — and it relates solely to processing a purchase, not to who you are.

2.Purchases & the app stores

StrikeArc Pro is sold through Apple's App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android). When you buy a subscription or the lifetime unlock, your payment is handled entirely by Apple or Google — StrikeArc and its developer never see your card number, billing address, or other payment details. Apple and Google act as the merchant of record and are governed by their own privacy policies (linked in the table below).

3.RevenueCat (subscription management)

StrikeArc uses RevenueCat to manage subscription entitlements — it is how the app knows a purchase succeeded and unlocks Pro, and how "Restore Purchases" works if you reinstall the app or switch devices. RevenueCat receives:

  • Purchase history, receipt, and transaction data from Apple/Google (product ID, price, renewal dates)
  • An anonymous RevenueCat app-user ID generated on your device — not your name or email
  • Basic device, platform (iOS/Android), and country information needed to process the purchase

This data is used only for: entitlement management (unlocking Pro), restoring purchases, fraud prevention, and aggregate analytics (e.g. how many people upgrade) — never for advertising or profiling you personally.

5.No sale of data

We do not sell, rent, or trade any data to third parties, and we do not run advertising in StrikeArc. Data described above is used strictly to make purchases and Pro entitlement work.

6.Data retention

Local app data (settings, shot history) stays on your device until you delete the app or clear its data — we never receive a copy. Purchase-related records held by RevenueCat, Apple, and Google are retained by those processors for as long as needed for entitlement, accounting, tax, and fraud-prevention purposes, per their own retention policies.

7.Your rights (GDPR / Norway)

If you are in the EU/EEA or Norway, you have rights under GDPR (and Norway's Personal Data Act) including the right to access, correct, or delete any purchase-related data associated with your anonymous RevenueCat ID, and the right to object to or restrict processing.

How to exercise these rights: email sivertskotvold@gmail.com with your platform (iOS/Android) and approximate purchase date. Since StrikeArc has no account system, we'll help locate and action your request via RevenueCat using the identifiers available. You can also manage or cancel your own subscription anytime from your Apple ID or Google Play account settings, and request deletion of payment data directly from Apple or Google.

8.Children

StrikeArc is not directed at children and is not intended for use by anyone under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in their country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9.Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of StrikeArc after a change means you accept the revised policy.
